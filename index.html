Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 9, 2025

Thank you for using the Injury Tracker App. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the information you enter into the app, such as injury details and personal notes. No personal identity information (like name or email) is required unless you choose to provide it.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used only to display your own logs and help you track your recovery. We do not share or sell your information to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Data is stored securely on your device or in your personal account if you sign in. We use trusted third-party services (like Firebase) to manage storage and authentication.

4. Your Rights

You can delete your logs at any time from the app. If using a sign-in option, you can also delete your account and all related data.

5. Contact

If you have any questions, please contact us at support@example.com.

This policy may be updated as needed. Please check back for any changes.
